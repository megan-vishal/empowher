Our Intent

Gender disparity in top-level positions has been a prevalent issue for centuries, and it may seem impossible to ignite change without already holding a seat in those executive boardrooms. However, as individual consumers, we have the power to seek out and support women-owned businesses with creators who understand the needs of women and are developing products with those perspectives in mind. The first step that we can all take to empower women in leadership positions starts with supporting women-owned businesses. To be a part of the change, take a look at these inspiring women and their businesses below!
